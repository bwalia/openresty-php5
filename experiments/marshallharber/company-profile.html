Company Profile

Marshall Harber is the U. K.'s most dynamic domestic staff  placement agency and our head office is based in Kensington, London. Our consultants are experienced in dealing with any request and can provide information that will assist you in finding either the most suitable person for the position you are offering, or for the position you are seeking.

Our role is to work closely with our clients and candidates, in complete confidentiality, enabling us to provide the best possible options. An accurate and detailed job description is vital in the initial recruitment procedure; therefore, we would also be happy to visit your home to make a complete assessment of your full requirements.

Marshall Harber's standards are exceptionally high and it is our policy to make an introduction that will lead to a committed and long-term working relationship.

Jo Harber - Founder

Jo was born and educated in the UK before moving to Kenya where she established and ran her own successful sales business.

In 1989, she relocated with her husband and two sons to South Africa where she developed a career in marketing and selling exclusive real estate.

In 2000, Jo moved back to the UK, having been offered the position of Executive Housekeeper & Private Secretary to a prominent London family, eventually leading to the offer of a consultant's position with a leading domestic staff placement agency.

Jo has an excellent track record in the recruitment and placement of top quality housekeepers and nannies, her reputation being built on her experience in understanding the value of providing a discreet, professional service with a natural and sympathetic understanding of her clients' requirements.

Jo has a keen interest in alternative health and nutrition and is a qualified Yoga instructor.

Alex Facta - Managing Director

Alex was raised and educated in South Africa where he studied Economics and Management Science at the University of Stellenbosch before relocating back to the UK in 2002.

Upon returning to the UK, Alex commenced a career in web design and IT support where he developed and ran his own small design firm. Alex provided IT support services to Marshall Harber before eventually striking up a partnership with the agency in 2004 which saw him go on to become a major share holder and eventually the Managing Director.

Having worked in the household staffing industry since 2004 he has acquired a solid understanding of the private staffing sector and is aware that every household is unique and no two vacancies are the same. He has ushered Marshall Harber into a new era of recruitment, not only leveraging the use of current technology to ensure the agency is effective and efficient but also taking each unique situation into account to ensure that long standing working relationships are built between clients and candidates.

Alex is a keen motorsport enthusiast and can regularly be found spending weekends on track days or out karting.

Carrie Gallagher - Recruitment Consultant

Carrie is an accomplished House Manager and Butler with an extensive catalogue of experience working for high profile and prestigious clients both overseas and across the UK. She started her career in the Royal Air Force and served for 10 years which included several tours overseas. Since then she has gone on to work in numerous prestigious and high profile private households within the UK and internationally as a House Manager and/or Butler. Carrie has worked for two Royal households in the capacity of Manager. One of these posts here in the UK for the British Royal family and another for the Royal Family based in Qatar.

Carrie's experience in private service and managing high profile estates around the world has perfectly equipped her with the knowledge and resources to effectively recruit staff for all the many different classes of private home.

Carrie loves to travel and experience new cultures, she has an active interest in animal welfare and is particularly interested in elephant conservation.

Beth Mays - Recruitment Consultant

Kyla is a highly organised and efficient administrator. She keeps Marshall Harber running smoothly and is an invaluable asset to the agency.

Kyla originally trained as a Beautician from college but went on to work in office based environments and quickly built a wealth of experience in company administration.

Having worked in various Admin roles, she was eventually offered a position at Marshall Harber in 2014 as an admin assistant. Kyla has progressed to become the Senior Administrator and is now responsible for the smooth day to day running of the agency.

Kyla is still a keen Beautician and she spends much of her spare time carrying out beauty treatments for friends and family.

Kyla McKenzie-Pryce - Administrator & Office Manager

Beth is a dedicated and hardworking professional, starting her career in South Africa with a strong Hospitality background and has been instrumental in recruitment and training at a variety of staffing levels. Beth moved into household management while still in South Africa and after relocating to the UK was offered a fabulous position on a country estate, in a role that combined exacting standards, a hard work ethic and confidentiality. This was a position she held and enjoyed for a number of years before moving into recruitment with Marshall harber.

Her passion for people and their development as well as for service standards and delivering a consistently superior service to our clientele ensures Beth is perfectly equiped to recruit for any private household Beth enjoys time with family and likes entertaining.
