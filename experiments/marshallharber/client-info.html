Clients Info

Our consultants at Marshall Harber are aware that every client's situation is unique. We will therefore work closely with you to meet your specific requirements.

Our database consists of only the most experienced private house professionals and our confidence lies in our ability to provide our clients with exactly what they are looking for. With the incorporation of 1st Agency of Mayfair, we are able to offer one of the largest selections of domestic staff to our clients.

Meeting Your Requirements...

To help us meet your exact requirements, we must build a very clear and accurate job description, so it is important that you provide us with details on your home, the duties, responsibilities you would expect and also the proposed hours and salary. This can take time, but it is vital that we have as much information as possible about the position you are offering. This will enable us to present you with the most suitable candidates for the role.

Contact our offices to initiate the procedure and one of our consultants will be happy to discuss your requirements. We also provide an "at home" consultation service. Please refer to Our Services for more information.

Making Your Selection

Once we have established your requirements, you will be presented with details of a selection of suitable candidates. When you have made your selection, please contact our offices to arrange interviews. Again, it is important, during the interviews, to be very specific about your requirements and to give the candidates a clear picture of what can be expected of them. We would be happy to provide you with our 'Guideline on Interviewing' should you require it.

When you feel confident that you have found the right person and would like to offer the position to the candidate, please contact your consultant who will in turn contact the candidate with your offer. We are also able to provide you with our 'Standard Draft Contract' that can be tailored to suit your needs.

We understand that finding the right person can take time, therefore you are under no obligation to make a quick decision and we are always available to answer any questions you might have.
